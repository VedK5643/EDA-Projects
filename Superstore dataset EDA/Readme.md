# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Overview
EDA on the Sample Superstore dataset to uncover insights about sales, profit, and customer behavior across US regions.

## Dataset
- 9994 orders, 21 columns
- Source: Sample Superstore (Tableau)

## Questions Answered
1. Which region generates the most profit?
2. Which category/sub-category has highest sales?
3. Which customer segment is most profitable?
4. How do sales trend over time?
5. Does discount lead to loss in profit?

## Key Findings
- West region most profitable (~108K), Central underperforms
- Phones highest sales, Copiers most profitable
- Consumer segment contributes ~50% of total profits
- Sales peaked in 2017 after dip in 2015
- Higher discounts consistently hurt profitability

## Tools
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
