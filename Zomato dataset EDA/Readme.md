# Zomato Bangalore Restaurant Analysis

## Overview
EDA on Zomato's Bangalore restaurant dataset to uncover insights about cuisines, ratings, locations, and cost patterns.

## Dataset
- 51,000+ restaurants, 17 columns
- Source: [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rajeshrampure/zomato-dataset)

## Questions Answered
1. Which locations have the most restaurants?
2. What is the distribution of ratings?
3. Does online ordering affect ratings?
4. Which restaurant types are most common?
5. What are the most popular cuisines?
6. Does cost affect ratings?

## Key Findings
- Whitefield has highest restaurant density, followed by BTM and Electronic City
- Most restaurants rated between 3.5-4.0, with 3.7 being most common
- Online ordering slightly improves ratings
- Quick Bites is the most common restaurant type
- North Indian and Chinese dominate cuisines
- Cost has no clear correlation with ratings

## Tools
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
